This chapter presents a detailed timeline of the steps to be accomplished in the master's program until this dissertation's defense. The first semester of regular enrollment started in January 2022. The detailed list of steps and activities is presented below and Table \ref{tab:timeline} presents the months when each step was executed.

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Attend the courses of the master's program;
    \begin{itemize}
        \item (Special student) Requirements Engineering;
        \item (1st semester) Theory of Computation \& Software Engineering 1;
        \item (2nd semester) Fundamentals of Computer Systems \& Software Engineering 2;
    \end{itemize}
    \item Investigate the research area and delimit the research scope: publication in ICAART;
    \item Plan and conduct the SLR;
    \item Report the SLR;
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Attempt to publish in CHASE: out of scope;
    \end{itemize}
    \item Develop the guide: GCCD v1.0;
    \item Validate GCCD v1.0 with a survey targeted at technology practitioners;
    \item Improve the paper with the validation and CHASE reviews;
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Attempt to publish in the Journal of Systems and Software: out of scope;
        \item Attempt to publish in Information and Software Technology: out of scope;
        \item Attempt to publish in the International Journal of Human Computer Interaction (IJHCI): accepted; 
    \end{itemize}
    \item Do review rounds with IJHCI;
    \item Extend the IJHCI paper into the dissertation;
    \item Improve the guide: GCCD v2.0;
    \item Qualify for the master's degree;
    \item Incorporate suggestions from the examination board;
    \item Verify GCCD v2.0 with a survey target at potential chatbot users;
    \item Report the verification's results into the dissertation;
    \item Report the verification's results into a paper;
    \item Defend the master's degree;
    \item Incorporate suggestions from the examination board;
\end{enumerate}
